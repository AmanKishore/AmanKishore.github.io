Recklessly Ambitious

Last updated: Oct 31, 2024

Forget the Mom Test, focus on ideas that sounds ridiculous to your mom.

"Live in the future, then build what's missing."

Let's assume AGI is 3 years away. What do you believe will exist in 5 years but most people would laugh at?

With technology advancing so quickly, it's simply not ambitious enough to build something that the current models do well.

Build for the future where models replace not augment.
