The Audacity of the Beginner

Last updated: Jul 1, 2026

It is the beginner who questions why things are the way they are
Confused by how the game works yet plays anyway

It is the beginner who stands in a line and asks why
When the backdoor seems wide open

It is the beginner who creates the new
While the expert is focused on improving the old

It is the beginner who is blissfully ignorant
As they step into a world of complexity

Finally, it is the beginner who wins
It must have been beginners luck
