Long Walks

Last updated: January 15, 2025

Honestly, I'm surprised it took me so long to write about walks.

Long walks are idea-generating machines. My biggest decisions happen as a direct result of walking with close friends. In SF, we'll walk miles along the Embarcadero and through North Beach, constantly iterating on whatever idea we're discussing.

Ironically, every late-night walk with friends sparks a realization or decision that changes the trajectory of at least one of our lives.

Yet I don't walk enough.

I've yet to hit diminishing marginal returns from going on walks with friends. My goal in 2025 is to find out if there's any real limit to the value I get from walking.

So I'm going on more walks.

If you feel like you're ready to make some life-altering decisions, let's go on a walk.

Who knows? We'll probably be different people than the ones who started.
