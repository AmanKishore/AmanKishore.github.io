Big If True: Prompters

Last updated: July 29, 2024

How does your day look when 50% of your time is spent interacting with AI?

AI is clearly disrupting our work and relationships. From AI coworkers (Devin) to AI friends (character.ai), the "people" we interact with daily is rapidly changing.

It's not obvious to me that we will interact with AGI as we do with humans. What communication will look like is an open question few companies are solving today.

This current paradigm of "prompt engineering" is the very beginnings of how we will be interacting with our fellow AI friends and co-workers.

Hopefully my friends at Promptlayer can figure it out.
