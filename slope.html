Slippery Slope

Last updated: July 9, 2024

Give me 2 minutes to convince you that every decision should be made by measuring the slope.

Slopes in Life:

  • Career:
    • take the new job if you think your slope will be higher there
    • quit the job if your slope ever goes negative
  • Relationships:
    • is your personal slope with your partner higher?
    • Is the relationship slope positive? flat? negative?
  • Business:
    • growth slowing? focus on distribution
    • decreasing retention? focus on product
  • City:
    • does your city support your growth? what would increase it?

Measure slope, it makes every decision easier.
